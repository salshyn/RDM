Packing Materials & Pricing

The packing service is billed by labor (hourly), and supplies are charged separately. Here's the rough idea so there are no surprises:

  • Boxes (S/M/L) — $5 each (well below the $8–$15 you'll find elsewhere)
  • Wardrobe boxes — included free (4–6 per truck)
  • Tape, paper, bubble wrap, shrink wrap — included with the packing service
  • Packing labor — billed by hour, with crew size matched to your home

For a typical 2-bedroom apartment, full packing usually takes a single packer most of a day. Larger homes get a 2-person team to keep things moving.

Packing FAQs

Common questions

Do I need full packing or just partial?
Depends on your time and energy. Most customers do partial packing — we handle the kitchen, fragiles, and hard-to-pack items, and they handle the easy stuff. If you're short on time or just want it off your plate entirely, full packing is the way.
How far in advance should I book?
As early as possible — Norah books up fast, especially for larger homes. For a typical 2-bedroom packing job, 2–3 weeks notice is ideal. Last-minute? Call us anyway, we'll do our best.
Can you pack just the kitchen or just the fragiles?
Absolutely. Partial packing is one of our most-requested services. The kitchen alone often takes 3–4 hours — most customers find it's the most miserable room to pack themselves.
Do you provide the boxes and supplies?
Yes. Boxes (S/M/L) are $5 each, and tape, paper, bubble wrap, and shrink wrap are included with the packing service. Wardrobe boxes (4–6 per truck) are free.
What about really delicate or expensive items?
We handle pianos, antique vases, art, mirrors, instruments, and other high-value items with extra care. Mention them when you request the quote so we plan the right materials and crew.
Do you offer unpacking too?
Yes — though most customers prefer to unpack themselves so they can decide where things go. If you want help, just ask. We can unpack and break down the empty boxes for hauling.
Will the packers also do the moving?
Often, yes — same crew or coordinated handoff. If we pack the day before, the moving crew arrives ready to load. Everything is in-house, no third parties.
